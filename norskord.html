🇳🇴 What is NorskOrd?
NorskOrd is a Norwegian vocabulary builder powered by AI. Look up any word or phrase and get a full linguistic breakdown including translation, IPA pronunciation, all word forms, grammar notes and example sentences — in your own language.
⚷ How to connect your AI (one-time setup)
1
The demo words work right away with no setup. To look up any word, you connect your own AI once.
2
Get an API key from any provider you like — Anthropic (Claude), OpenAI (ChatGPT), Google (Gemini), or any OpenAI-compatible service.
3
Click ⚷ Connect AI in the top-right, pick your provider, and paste the key. That's it.
4
Your key is saved only in your browser and is sent only to the provider you chose. Your word bank and progress also save automatically.
⌕ Lookup tab
Type any Norwegian or English word or phrase. Toggle EN/NO to tell the app which language you're typing in. Hit Look up or press Enter. Save words you want to remember using the + Save button.
⊡ My Words tab
All your saved words in one place. Filter by verb, noun, adjective or phrase. Tap any word to see its full entry again. Use Export JSON to back up your word bank — copy the text and save it as a .json file.
◈ HFW Progress tab
Track your progress through the 500 most common Norwegian words. After each study session, log how far you've reached. Use Copy to chat to paste your progress into your AI chat so you can continue exactly where you left off.
❞ Idioms tab
Look up specific idioms, generate a random Norwegian idiom by theme, or type an English idiom to find its Norwegian equivalent. Save the ones you want to remember.
🌍 Change language
Click the language button in the top right corner to change the language all entries are explained in. Supports English, Polish, Arabic, Somali, Tigrinya, Dari, Urdu, Spanish and French.
